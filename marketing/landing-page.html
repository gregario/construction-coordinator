Questions

  • Is this for general contractors or owner-builders?

    Owner-builders managing their own residential new construction. If you're coordinating trades and materials on a single build and you already understand construction sequencing, this is built for your workflow.

  • Do I need to install anything?

    No. Construction Manager is a web app that installs to your phone's home screen like a native app. Works on any modern browser -- desktop for planning, mobile for the job site.

  • What happens to my data if I cancel?

    Export your full project as JSON, CSV, or PDF at any time, including after cancellation. Your data is never held hostage.

  • How does the cascade scheduling work?

    Every task has dependencies and durations. When you adjust a date or update a material lead time, the engine recalculates every downstream task automatically. You see the new timeline immediately.

  • Can I import my existing schedule?

    Launch includes CSV import. If your schedule lives in a spreadsheet, you can bring it over. Custom import from other tools is on the roadmap.
