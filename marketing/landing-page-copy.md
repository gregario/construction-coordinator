## FAQ

### Is this for general contractors or owner-builders?

Owner-builders managing their own residential new construction. If you're coordinating trades and materials on a single build and you already understand construction sequencing, this is built for your workflow. General contractors managing multiple simultaneous projects should look at Buildertrend or Procore.

### Do I need to install anything?

No. Construction Manager is a web app that installs to your phone's home screen like a native app (it's a PWA). Works on any modern browser -- desktop for planning, mobile for the job site.

### What happens to my data if I cancel?

You can export your full project as JSON, CSV, or PDF at any time, including after cancellation. Your data is never held hostage.

### How does the cascade scheduling work?

Every task has dependencies and durations. When you mark a task complete, adjust a date, or update a material lead time, the engine recalculates every downstream task automatically. You see the new timeline immediately -- no manual spreadsheet updates.

### Can I import my existing project schedule?

Launch support includes import from CSV files. If your current schedule lives in a spreadsheet, you can bring it over. Custom import from other tools is on the roadmap.
